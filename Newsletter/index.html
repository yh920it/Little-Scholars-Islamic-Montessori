🌼 Program Update

Summer learning begins July 6th

This summer, Little Scholars Islamic Montessori will be offering a structured, mindful, and joyful program for children ages 6 months to 7 years. Each day will include a blend of faith, language, movement, memorization, and reading in a way that is natural and age appropriate.

July 6 Program Begins

A summer rooted in faith, fun, and growth

Families can look forward to a balanced summer rhythm that includes Arabic, Quran, sports and physical activities, Hifth, Adhkaar, and reading. Younger children will be guided with gentle care and simple exposure, while older children will enjoy more structured learning, movement, and memorization practice.
