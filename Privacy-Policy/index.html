Privacy Policy for
Our School Community

At Little Scholars Islamic Montessori, we value the trust families place in us. This page explains how information submitted through our website may be collected, used, and protected.

Last Updated

June 2, 2026. This policy is written for clear family communication and should be reviewed periodically as the website and enrollment process grow.

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Little Scholars Educational Services LLC may collect and use information submitted through this website, including enrollment inquiries, callback requests, and general communication forms.

This page is not a substitute for legal advice. A licensed attorney should review the final policy before public launch if you need legal compliance language for your exact operations.

Information We May Collect

Families may voluntarily provide information when completing an enrollment inquiry or contacting the school.

  • Parent or guardian name, phone number, and email address
  • Child’s name, age, gender, program interest, and desired start date
  • Potty-training status, allergies, medical notes, developmental notes, and other family-provided details
  • Preferred days and times for a callback
  • Any additional comments or questions submitted by the family

How We Use Information

Information submitted through the website is used to review enrollment inquiries, determine whether a callback may be appropriate, communicate with families, answer questions, and support the admissions process.

We may also use contact information to follow up regarding tours, availability, program fit, rates, or next steps requested by the family.

How Information Is Shared

Little Scholars Islamic Montessori does not sell family information. Information may be shared only when necessary to operate the school, respond to a family inquiry, comply with legal obligations, or protect the safety and well-being of children, families, staff, or the school community.

Children’s Information

Information about children is treated with care and should only be submitted by a parent or legal guardian. Families should avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive information unless it is important for enrollment review, safety, health, or care planning.

Security and Retention

We aim to handle family information responsibly and limit access to those who need it for school communication or enrollment review. No website or electronic transmission can be guaranteed completely secure, so families should contact us directly if they prefer not to submit certain details online.

Information may be retained as long as needed for enrollment review, school communication, recordkeeping, or legal and operational purposes.

Questions or Updates

Families may contact us to ask questions about this policy, update submitted information, or request that we review information previously provided through the website.
